Nicotine Calculator

Nicotine Base Strength refers to the high strength nicotine base that you'll be mixing into your eliquid.

Target Strength refers to the nicotine strength (in mg) that you would like your eLiquid to be.

Batch Size refers to the amount of eLiquid (in mL) you will be mixing. If you are mixing a full bottle, enter the bottle size.
